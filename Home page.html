Historical overview

Despite finishing behind Barcelona on the La Liga table in 2022/23,
Real Madrid had the last laugh The club reported a record total revenue of $873 million, the highest in the world, and an 8% increase year-over-year.
The club's biggest boost came from a $64 million increase in commercial revenue, along with a $29 million increase in matchday revenue.
In an effort to further increase it's revenue lead, Real Madrid announced a new partnership with HP that will feature the tech company's logo as the first ever to appear on the sleeve of Real Madrid's jerseys. According to reports, the deal could be worth up to $75.7 million annually.
